About Us

Our Story

DisCover was born from a passion to bring the exciting world of Ultimate Frisbee to Brunei. We believe that sport has the power to unite communities, spark creativity, and build lasting friendships.


Our Mission: To grow frisbee in Brunei by offering workshops that mix creativity, play, and empathy — giving participants fun experiences and meaningful lessons that extend far beyond the field.


Through our innovative approach, we're not just teaching a sport; we're building a community that values teamwork, creativity, and personal growth.
